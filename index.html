Paylert Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 07/04/2026

Paylert ("we", "our", or "us") is a UPI payment notification assistant that helps users receive voice alerts for incoming payment notifications from apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, and similar services.

1. Information We Access

Paylert accesses device notifications with user permission. This includes notification content from UPI payment apps such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and others.

This may include:

  • Sender name (if available in notification)
  • Transaction amount
  • Payment status messages
  • App source (e.g., GPay, PhonePe)
  • Notification timestamp

2. How We Use Information

  • To detect incoming UPI payment notifications
  • To generate real-time voice alerts
  • To store transaction history for user reference
We DO NOT:
  • Access your bank account or UPI PIN
  • Collect OTPs or sensitive banking credentials
  • Require or store your phone number
  • Sell or share your data with third parties

3. Data Storage

Payment-related notification data is securely stored in our cloud database for user history and tracking purposes. Users can view and manage their stored transaction history within the app.

4. Account System

Paylert uses an email-based login system. We do not require or collect phone numbers for registration or authentication.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as Firebase and advertising providers (e.g., Google AdMob). These services may collect limited data as per their own privacy policies.

6. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect stored data. However, no system is completely secure.

7. User Control

  • You can disable notification access anytime in device settings
  • You can delete stored data from within the app (if available)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the app or on this page.
